Non Pregnancy Related

  • Age > 35 years
  • Minority ethic group
  • Vulnerable socio-economic background
  • Obesity
  • Diabetes, including gestational diabetes
  • Recent surgery
  • Symptoms of inection in the past week
  • Immunocompromised e.g. Systemic Lupus
  • Chronic renal failure/liver failure/heart failure
